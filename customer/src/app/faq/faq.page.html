I can't login. What now?

To better assist you, please follow these steps:

1. **Password Reset**: If you've forgotten your password, you can initiate a password reset by clicking on the "Forgot Password" link on our login page. Follow the instructions sent to your registered email address to create a new password.

2. **Username Verification**: Make sure you are using the correct username or email address associated with your account during login. Double-check for any typos.

3. **Browser Compatibility**: Ensure you are using a compatible web browser with updated versions. Clear your browser's cache and cookies and try logging in again.

4. **Account Lockout**: After multiple unsuccessful login attempts, your account may be temporarily locked for security reasons. Wait for a while and try logging in again.

If you continue to experience difficulties after following these steps, please reply to this email: ItsPersonal@gmail.com We will get back to you as soon as possible.

To expedite the process, please provide the following information when reaching out to us:

- Your registered email address or username

- Any error messages you receive during login
