Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How can I Personalize my Order

After browsing through the shop, click the plus attached to the inventory item. Once clicked you'll a page where you can add your design image and select your colour.

How Can i request a refund?

First, take a look at the refund policy to ensure you qualify. Then, if you qualify, head over to the "Request Refund" page. Here you must enter a refund reason and submit your response. Once your response has been sent, you will receive a notification stating that your refund has been received.

When will I receive my refund?

If your refund has been accepted, you can expect to receive the refund in the next 10 days.

How can I get in contact with someone from "It's Personal"?

Simply visit the "Contact Us" page, here you must enter your name, email and leave a comment.

What is the refund policy?

The refund policy can be found in the "Refunds" tab

What is the delivery options?

We use external courier services.

Where are you located?

Our head office is located in Edenvale, Johannesburg. Most operations are done online however.
