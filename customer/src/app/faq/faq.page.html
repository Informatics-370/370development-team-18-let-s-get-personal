Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How can I personalize my Order?

In the basket page select the personalisation button.
Please note we do require bothe the text you would like and an inspiration photo. Once clicked you'll a page where you can add your design image and select your colour.


What are the delivery options?

When placing your order you will be directed to a page where you can choose what delivery company you would like to use.
Please note we only delivery within South Africa.


What are our payment options?

We are currently only using payfast as an option as it is secure and reliable.


Where are we located?

Our head office is located in Edenvale, Johannesburg.

How can I request a refund?

First, take a look above at the refund policy to ensure you qualify. Then, if you qualify, please send an email to itspersonal@gmail.com.


When will I receive my refund?

If your refund has been accepted, you can expect to receive the refund in the next 5 working days.


How can I get in contact with someone from "It's Personal"?

Simply visit the "Contact Us" page, here you must enter your name, email and leave a comment.
